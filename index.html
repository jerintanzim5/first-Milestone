Our Donate Plan

Our donate plan-We know to make the donation process reach people in three steps.

Food For a family-

We will distribute dry food,pure drinking water and medicines to alleviate the hunger of the people

Shelter For Family-

We will build houses for the victims who do not have a place to keep their heads with the help of the local administration.

Education For a Child-

Teachers will be sent every week to teach so that the children do not fall behind in their studies as the school is closed.

Our work is only possible with the generous support of our donors and sponsors. 100% of all your donations go immediately to our projects on the ground to support the guardians of our forests to protect our planet and its biodiversity, which our lives depend on.
